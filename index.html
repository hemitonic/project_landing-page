Chinchillas are fluffy creatures

Chinchillas are low-maintenance, soft, and playfully curious pets. They have traditionally been valued for their dense fur, which grows at 60 hairs per follicle. Chinchilla enthusiasts will tell you that with the right care these round little rodents make great pets.

Chinchillas aren't your average rodent.

Chinchillas are notably active and exuberant. They love to run, jump, climb, and explore.

Domesticated chinchillas can live for up to 20 years

Chinchillas are notoriously picky eaters! Given the option between multiple types of food, chinchillas will usually select only their favorite morsels and discard the rest.

Chinchillas can leap over six feet high. Some pet chinchillas have even been reported to jump onto the top of refrigerators!
